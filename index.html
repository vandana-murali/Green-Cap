The Challenge

Why Scope 3 Is Still Broken

Despite regulatory pressure and climate commitments, corporate Scope 3 emissions remain fragmented, unverifiable, and impossible to trace at the supplier level.

Fragmented Supplier Data

Emissions data exists in disconnected spreadsheets, PDFs, and surveys across hundreds of suppliers. No standardization, no granularity, no part-level traceability.

Survey-Based Guesswork

Legacy carbon tools rely on supplier surveys and industry averages ‚Äî leading to inaccurate, non-auditable emissions data that fails PCAF and CSRD compliance standards.

SMEs Lack Resources

Small and medium suppliers are overwhelmed by complex reporting requirements. Without automated tools, they can't meet buyer sustainability mandates or access green financing.

No Finance Integration

Existing tools don't integrate with ERP/MES systems or financial workflows, making it impossible to embed emissions data into procurement, risk management, and sustainable finance decisions.
